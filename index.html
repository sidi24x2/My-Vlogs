The Day I Saw Her

Ever felt seeing someone that they are different from the rest. We being human do want something or someone different from the others. There are those who are addicted to drugs and there are those who are addicted to money, some to love many to alcohol but I'm sure that no-one is as addicted towards her as am I.

A group where I can see all the people with no affection there was she lost in her thoughts, looking dumb with a slight hopelessness. She was lost in here own world like none of the rest matters to her. I looked at her and as they say you just need one reason to stay if you want to.

Among a crowd of open haired girls she was giving a classical look. Her hair were tied. I got stuck for a moment.
